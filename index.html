Featured Projects

Clear problem framing, method selection, and measurable outcomes.

Early Diabetes Risk Prediction

Built an early screening model to flag high-risk individuals using population-level health survey data.

  • Problem: Class imbalance and noisy behavioral/clinical predictors in BRFSS data.
  • Approach: Feature engineering plus SMOTE with Random Forest on 253k records.
  • Outcome: Reached approximately 85% accuracy for early risk stratification.

NBA Game Outcome Forecasting

Developed forecasting models for game-level performance metrics across more than 1,100 NBA games.

  • Problem: Multi-output prediction where team-level dynamics shift week to week.
  • Approach: Time-aware feature construction and regression model comparison.
  • Outcome: Achieved R squared near 0.84 with MAE below 1 on key targets.

Billiards Regression Study

Investigated in-game performance drift and shot accuracy patterns in competitive 9-ball play.

  • Problem: Quantify how player accuracy changes across match phases.
  • Approach: Regression analysis on 150 games with segment-wise trend modeling.
  • Outcome: Identified a repeatable mid-game increase in shot precision.

Evolution of NBA Offense

Longitudinal analysis of four decades of league trends to explain modern offensive strategy.

  • Problem: Determine which metrics became strongest predictors of team success.
  • Approach: Era-adjusted comparisons and trend decomposition across seasons.
  • Outcome: Showed three-point efficiency overtook defense-only metrics in impact.

Crime and SAT Score Analysis

Explored links between state-level crime patterns and education performance outcomes.

  • Problem: Separate meaningful social signal from aggregate-level noise.
  • Approach: Correlation and regression diagnostics across crime categories.
  • Outcome: Found moderate negative correlation with violent crime, weaker effect otherwise.
