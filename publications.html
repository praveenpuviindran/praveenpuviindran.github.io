Publications & Abstracts

The Perceived Usability of Virtual Visits Among Black Adults Receiving Oncology Care

Oncologist, 2023

Qualitative study analyzing the communication barriers and preferences surrounding telehealth usage among Black oncology patients. Identified trust and nonverbal communication concerns influencing virtual visit satisfaction.

DOI: 10.1200/OP.2023.00431

Black Adults’ Perceptions of Virtual Visits in Oncology Care

Journal of Clinical Oncology, 2022

Mixed-methods evaluation of telehealth usability and patient preferences in oncology care settings, highlighting disparities in technology trust and communication needs.

DOI: 10.1200/JCO.2022.01412

Function of HERVH in The Regulation of hESC Growth

Abstract (2019)

Early work demonstrating the functional impact of LTR7/HERVH regulatory sequences on stem cell proliferation dynamics.
