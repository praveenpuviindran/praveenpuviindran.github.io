Projects

A collection of my major data science, machine learning, and analytics work across clinical, biomedical, and sports intelligence domains.

Flagship Project

NBA Synergy Engine (Moneyball 2.0)

A permutation-invariant DeepSet neural network system that predicts lineup chemistry and identifies optimal roster fits using 11 seasons of player tracking data (2014–2025). Offers analytical tooling for synergy-based roster construction.

  • Engineered ETL pipelines ingesting 170k+ possessions into SQL
  • Used GMM + PCA to define 10 modern playstyle archetypes
  • Implemented a PyTorch DeepSet model to predict net-rating outcomes
  • Created a generative optimizer (“AI GM”) simulating 450+ players

Undergraduate Projects

Early Diabetes Risk Prediction

Analyzed 253,680 BRFSS records to identify predictors of diabetes onset. Built Random Forest classifiers with SMOTE-balancing achieving 85% accuracy.

  • Feature engineering (blood pressure, BMI, cholesterol)
  • QWK = 0.85
  • SHAP-based misclassification analysis

NBA Game Outcome Forecasting

Built Random Forest + regression models to forecast 1,100+ NBA games with R² = 0.837 and MAE < 1 on offensive rebounds and total points.

In-Depth Analysis of 9-Ball Billiards

Statistical modeling of 150+ games identifying mid-game pockets as 16% more accurate than early-phase play. No statistical advantage based on break performance.

Evolution of NBA Offense & Defense (1980–2020)

Regression analysis over 40 years of NBA statistics showing 3PT efficiency contributes 30% more to team success than defense in the modern era.

Crime & School Infrastructure Impact on SAT Scores

Modeled 100+ school districts, revealing a −0.45 correlation between violent crime and SAT performance, with negligible impact from non-violent crime.
