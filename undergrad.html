Undergraduate Research

Cancer Pharmacology — UNC School of Medicine (2023–2025)

Optimizing Chemotherapy Scheduling

I conducted 20-day lung cancer cell culture experiments testing different dosing schedules for Docetaxel/Paclitaxel in NCI-H2228 and HCC827 lines. Using R and Mathematica, I analyzed viability and recovery to identify cycles that reduce toxicity while preserving therapeutic effect.

Virology — UNC School of Data Science (2025)

Kaposi Sarcoma RNA-seq Subtype Classifier

Built a qPCR-ready subtype classifier trained on viral RNA-seq from HIV+ patients, integrating PCA, LASSO, Random Forests, SVMs, and MLPs (ROC-AUC 0.95). Identified a 6-gene signature using RFE and SHAP.

Neurology — Dynamic Causal Modeling (2024–2025)

Resting-State fMRI Directionality in Epilepsy

Using SPM and MATLAB, I analyzed directional influence between hippocampal heads to map seizure propagation patterns via DCM. Preprocessing included realignment and normalization of fMRI time series.

Radiation Oncology — MD Anderson (2024)

Skin Microbiome Shifts During Radiotherapy

Analyzed 16S rRNA samples using QIIME2 to quantify alpha/beta diversity, taxonomic shifts, and toxicity-associated microbial profiles. Conducted PERMANOVA and PCoA analyses to identify radiation-linked patterns.

Oncology Health Services — UNC Eshelman School of Pharmacy (2022–2023)

Telehealth in Oncology — Disparities & Patient Preferences

Conducted literature review of 50+ papers and qualitative coding of 49 interviews to evaluate virtual visit usability among Black adults receiving oncology care. Co-authored a peer-reviewed publication.

Molecular Biology & Genetics — Duke & UNC (2019–2022)

Protein Interaction Mapping & Stem Cell Regulation

Investigated MBD2 mutations affecting protein-protein interactions and studied LTR7 sequences regulating hESC proliferation (Duke). Early work exploring gene regulation fundamentals.
